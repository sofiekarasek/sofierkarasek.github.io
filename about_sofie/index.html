ABOUT SOFIE

Sofie Karasek is an anti-sexual violence activist and co-founder of the national organization End Rape on Campus. She spearheaded two 31-person federal complaints against the University of California, Berkeley, where she is currently a senior studying Political Economy. Sofie has also been a leading advocate for California's groundbreaking affirmative consent law, is a subject in the new documentary The Hunting Ground, and has been featured in national and international media including The Wall Street Journal, The Guardian, CNN, Bloomberg Businessweek, and Marie Claire.
